White Rabbit Studio

Graphic & Web Design Boutique

Ursala Hudson, the White Rabbit, collaborates with select companies to define and accentuate their brand in print design. She is passionate about helping others build or modernize their business through their visual appearance, and works closely with clients to create successful solutions in projecting their image.

As a Fine Artist working in an array of mediums, tangible graphic work can supplement any digital design project, or be the centering focus. Ursala is trained in pencil, ink & charcoal, acrylic paint, textiles, hand-pressed printmaking, clay, and digital & 35mm films.
